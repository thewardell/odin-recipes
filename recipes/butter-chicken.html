Butter Chicken

What is Butter Chicken?

Butter chicken is gud.

Ingredients

  • chicken thighs
  • garam masala
  • butter
  • cumin

Directions

  1. Put tomatoes and spices in instant pot.
  2. drop chicken on top.
  3. Cook for 10 minutes on high pressure.
  4. Remove chicken and blend tomates.
  5. Add butter and cream and saute to simmer.
  6. Break up chicken and add back to sauce.
  7. Serve over basmati rice.
